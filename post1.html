Artificial intelligence systems are only as fair as the data they’re trained on. This post explores how bias creeps into datasets and what designers can do to mitigate it.

From hiring tools to facial recognition, biased AI can reinforce systemic inequalities. Ethical design starts with awareness—and ends with accountability.
