Scenario: Resume Screening

You’re training a hiring model. You must choose which dataset to prioritize:

Option A: Elite Dataset

  • Resumes from top universities (IIT, Stanford, MIT)
  • High GPA, traditional experience
  • Low diversity (mostly male, urban, upper-class)

Option B: Inclusive Dataset

  • Resumes from bootcamps, community colleges, freelancers
  • Mixed experience, non-linear paths
  • High diversity (gender, geography, background)
